MedIX Papers

During the summer of 2006 I had the opportunity to take part in a research experience for undergrads (REU) program at DePaul university in Chicago called MedIX. It was an incredibly productive summer and during that time my teammates and I were able to both develop an open source medical imaging system called BRISC and publish several papers related to the system.

  • BRISC - An Open Source Pulmonary Nodule Image Retrieval Framework
  • A Lookup and Reference Tool for Pulmonary Computed Tomography Nodules
  • A Comparison of Texture Models for Automatic Liver Segmentation
  • Content-Based Image Retrieval for Pulmonary Computed Tomography Nodule Images
